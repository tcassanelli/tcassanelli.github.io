Research

My primary lines of research are radio--optical sub-second transients 游눤 and their localization, very-long-baseline interferometry (VLBI), radio--optical astronomical instrumentation, optical fast photon counters, and holography for radio telescopes 游니 (from centimeter and sub-millimeter wavelengths).
